Suspended Honeycomb

To inspire your professional journey, we are proud to share the Suspended Honeycomb, the masterpiece that led the Italian team to victory at the Coupe du Monde de la Pâtisserie 2021.

Led by Lorenzo Puca, Andrea Restuccia, and Massimo Pica, the team dedicated 18 months of rigorous training to perfect a dessert that truly captures the theme: All art is an imitation of Nature. By using honey as a common thread, they created a sensory journey through pollination, blending the nostalgic flavors of childhood with futuristic techniques. This plated dessert is proof of what can be achieved when you refuse to question your limits. Explore the award-winning recipe below and bring a piece of world class pastry to your kitchen.

Step by Step

❮ ❯

01

Honey comb wafer

Ingredients

  • 500 g water
  • 150 g shortcrust flour
  • 150 g rice flour
  • 200 g icing sugar
  • 75 g butter
  • 60 g fresh eggs
  • 0,5 g Bourbon vanilla

Procedure

Emulsify all the ingredients and proceed to make the wafers.

02

Apricot jelly for baking mold

Ingredients

  • 200 g apricot pulp
  • 5 g Bloom 120 animal gelatin
  • 30 g water for gelatin
  • 44 g dextrose

Procedure

Heat the pulp, with sugars and gelatin and pour.

03

Blancmange

Ingredients

  • 100 g milk
  • 4 g almond paste
  • 20 g dextrose
  • 10 g sugar
  • 2,25 g Bloom 120 animal gelatin
  • 11,2 g water for gelatin
  • 100 g cream
  • 2 g vanilla

Procedure

Warm up the 4 first ingredients, add the gelatine and add the soft whipped cream.

04

Passion fruit and Apricot sauce

Ingredients

  • 110 g apricot purée
  • 11 g passion fruit purée
  • 15 g sugar
  • 16 g dextrose
  • 0,3 g Bourbon vanilla

Procedure

Emulsify and filter all the ingredients.

05

Tutti Frutti crémeux

Ingredients

  • 50 g passion fruit pulp
  • 225 g apricot pulp
  • 19 g starch
  • 4 g Bloom 170 animal gelatin
  • 19 g water
  • 20 g water at 40°C

Procedure

Cook the first ingredients. Emulsify with water and gelatine. Use cold.

06

Honey ice cream

Ingredients

  • 593 g milk
  • 200 g cream
  • 125 g mixed blossom honey
  • 44 g milk powder
  • 2 g neutral
  • 64 g maltodextrin 19 DE
  • 30 g dehydrated glucose

Procedure

Cook all ingredients at 85°C and stir to obtain a creamy consistency. Churn. Extraction temperature -7°C.

07

Vanilla and lime ganache

Ingredients

  • 62,5 g water
  • 10 g dextrose
  • 2,5 g 120 bloom gelatin
  • 12,5 g water for gelatin
  • 50 g White Ivoire Chocolate
  • 125 g cream
  • 2 g vanilla
  • 3 g lime
  • 21 g raw almonds

Procedure

Bring to boil the water with, natural flavors and dextrose, add the gelatine and white chocolate. Emulsify with cold cream.

08

Mango compote

Ingredients

  • 68 g mango purée
  • 100 g fresh diced mango
  • 1,30 g pectin NH
  • 21 g granulated sugar

Procedure

Cook all the ingredients and bring to boil.

09

Almond sponge cake

Ingredients

  • 250 g whole eggs
  • 180 g caster sugar
  • 150 g almond powder
  • 65 g shortbread flour
  • 65 g butter
  • 1 g vanilla
  • 1 g lemon zest

Procedure

Whip the egg, sugar, and natural flavours, add the powders and the melted butter. Bake at 180°C for 7 minutes.
